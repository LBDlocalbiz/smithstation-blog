Pre-Storage RV Preparation Checklist

Essential Winterization Steps

Alabama winters, while mild, still require specific preparation based on our regional experience:

  • Empty all water tanks to prevent freeze damage during occasional sub-32°F nights
  • Remove all food items that attract local wildlife including raccoons and possums
  • Clean interior and exterior surfaces using RV-specific products
  • Check tire pressure and inflate to manufacturer specifications (cold weather reduces pressure 1-2 PSI)
  • Add fuel stabilizer to prevent gas degradation during 3+ month storage periods
  • Remove batteries or connect maintenance chargers to prevent sulfation

Climate-Specific Preparations for Smith Station

Smith Station's 65% average humidity demands additional moisture control measures:

  • Place DampRid or similar moisture absorbers in living areas (replace every 60 days)
  • Open cabinet doors for air circulation preventing mold growth
  • Cover mattresses and upholstery with breathable fabric covers
  • Inspect and seal roof penetrations - Alabama's temperature cycles cause expansion/contraction
